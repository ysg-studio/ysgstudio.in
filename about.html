About

Notes on looking closely.

I'm Yahya Gopalani, a photographer and visual artist working out of Mumbai.

My work moves across street, architecture, food, product, portrait, and commercial photography — not as separate disciplines, but as different ways of asking the same question: what makes a frame worth stopping on?

Most of what I shoot starts on the street — light falling somewhere it shouldn't, a gesture that lasts half a second, a building holding a conversation with the sky. That instinct for the unposed moment carries into everything else I do, including the commercial and brand work I take on for clients who want their imagery to feel alive rather than art-directed into stillness.

My photography has picked up recognition internationally, including through 35Awards, and has been viewed by millions of people across photography platforms. But the numbers are a side effect, not the point — the point is still just the next frame.

I work with brands, artists, and individuals on portrait sessions, campaign photography, and documentation work, based in Mumbai and available across India.
