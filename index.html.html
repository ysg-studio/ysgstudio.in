About

The studio

We shoot for people whose work already has energy — our job is to make sure the frame keeps up.

YSG Studio is a photography and visual production house founded by Yahya Gopalani, built around a single idea: every frame should carry energy, not just information. We work across portraiture, campaign visuals, and artist documentation.

We shoot for musicians, comedians, and brands who want their imagery to feel as alive as the work it represents — and we're building a trusted network of creators across India's major cities to take that standard beyond Mumbai.
